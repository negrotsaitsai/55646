  • Bhelpuri

    April 3, 2021

    This is a common authentic snack on the streets of Mumbai. The ingredients are crispy rice, peanuts, green peppers and fragrant tamarind sauce. The taste is slightly spicy and can be eaten directly or rolled in pancakes. Various ingredients bring a rich and balanced taste. The taste is so exciting, it is no wonder that it is one of the must-try Indian delicacies.

  • Lamb Curry

    This is a Bengali-style slow-cooked dish. Lamb and spices are cooked in a thick sauce made of onion, ginger and garlic. It is usually eaten on festive occasions. Calcutta especially likes it. This dish comes to celebrate the festive season. Lamb curry is cooked with mustard oil, which is the most commonly used cooking oil in West Bengal, which makes the dishes in Bengal have a different flavor.
